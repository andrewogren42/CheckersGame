Play against our AI checkers agent. On our website found at https://checkersgame-a3b92.web.app/ you can play against the AI agent that we are making for our Artifial Intelligence class on our fully custom made UI. The AI will have two heuritiscs to play at and as the player you will be able to toggle between them in the side bar. There are also buttons to reset the game, the scoreboard, change from a regular 10 minute game to a speed 3 minute game, dark mode will change the colors to a darker tone, and finally the legal moves toggle will show or hide the possible legal moves for a given piece after selecting it.
